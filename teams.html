Premier League

Cau lac bo thong tri bong da Anh trong thap ky qua duoi su dan dat cua HLV Pep Guardiola. San nha: Etihad Stadium.

Arsenal FC
Premier League

The Gunners dang trong giai doan phuc hung manh me duoi thoi HLV Mikel Arteta. San nha: Emirates Stadium.

Liverpool FC
Premier League

The Reds — mot trong nhung cau lac bo thanh cong nhat lich su bong da Anh. San nha: Anfield.

Chelsea FC
Premier League

The Blues voi lich su phong phu va doi hinh day sao. San nha: Stamford Bridge, London.

Manchester United
Premier League

Cau lac bo vi dai nhat nuoc Anh voi 20 chuc vo dich Premier League. San nha: Old Trafford.

Tottenham Hotspur
Premier League

The Spurs voi san van dong hien dai nhat nuoc Anh. San nha: Tottenham Hotspur Stadium.

La Liga

Real Madrid
La Liga

Cau lac bo vi dai nhat lich su Champions League voi 15 lan vo dich. San nha: Santiago Bernabeu.

FC Barcelona
La Liga

Blaugrana — bieu tuong cua bong da tan cong dep mat. San nha: Camp Nou, Barcelona.

Atletico Madrid
La Liga

Los Colchoneros noi tieng voi loi choi phong thu chac chan. San nha: Metropolitano.

Bundesliga

Bundesliga

Borussia Dortmund
Bundesliga

Bayer Leverkusen
Bundesliga

Die Werkself dang trong giai doan hoang kim duoi thoi HLV Xabi Alonso. San nha: BayArena.
